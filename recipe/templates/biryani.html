Chicken Biryani üçõ

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tbsp Garam Masala
  • 2 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander and Mint Leaves (chopped)

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice for 30 mins.
  2. Fry spices & onions until golden.
  3. Add chicken, yogurt, and spices ‚Äì cook till tender.
  4. Cook rice separately (70% done).
  5. Layer rice over chicken, top with coriander & mint.
  6. Cover, cook on low for 15 mins. Done! üçõ
