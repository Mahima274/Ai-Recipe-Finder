Chole Bhature

class="recipe-image">

Ingredients:

For Chole (Chickpea Curry):

  • 1 cup Chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 Onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tbsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tbsp Chole Masala
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 2 Cloves
  • 1-inch Cinnamon Stick
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

For Bhature:

  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour (Maida)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Instructions:

For Chole:

  1. Pressure cook the soaked chickpeas with salt and water for 3-4 whistles.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cloves, and cinnamon.
  3. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and tomato puree. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and chole masala.
  6. Add the boiled chickpeas along with some water and simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

For Bhature:

  1. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, yogurt, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Add water as needed and knead into a soft dough. Let it rest for 2 hours.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into circles.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the bhature until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with chole and enjoy!
