Fruit Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup kiwi, diced
  • 1 cup pineapple, diced
  • 1 cup grapes, halved
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine all the fruits.

2. In a small bowl, mix honey and lemon juice.

3. Drizzle the honey mixture over the fruits and toss gently.

4. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator.
