Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 clove Garlic, minced
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions

1. Boil the pasta according to package instructions.

3. Add the cooked pasta, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

4. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese on top.
